About the Theme

From Burden to Solution
— Safe Food Everywhere

World Food Safety Day, observed annually on 7th June, is a global platform to draw attention to and inspire action to prevent, detect, and manage foodborne risks.

The 2026 theme — "Safe Food Everywhere" — reflects a collective commitment to shift the narrative: food safety is not merely a compliance burden but a powerful driver of public health, economic growth, and sustainable food systems.
