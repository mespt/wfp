About the Theme

From Burden
to Solution - Safe Food Everywhere

World Food Safety Day, observed annually on 4th June, is a global platform to draw attention to and inspire action to prevent, detect, and manage foodborne risks.

The 2026 theme — "Safe Food Everywhere" — reflects a collective commitment to shift the narrative: food safety is not merely a compliance burden but a powerful driver of public health, economic growth, and sustainable food systems.

This EAC Regional Webinar unites governments, regulatory bodies, industry, academia, and civil society to share knowledge and forge regional solutions.
